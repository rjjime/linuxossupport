Cookie Policy

Enterprise Linux Solutions LLC is committed to transparency about how we use cookies on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt out of these cookies without affecting how the site functions.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information. This data helps us improve our website's performance and user experience.

  • Purpose: Track page views, session duration, and user behavior
  • Data Collected: IP address (anonymized), browser type, device information
  • Retention: 26 months

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements. They may share data with third-party advertising partners.

  • Purpose: Personalized advertising and retargeting
  • Data Collected: Browsing history, interests, and preferences
  • Retention: 13 months

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced features and personalization, such as remembering your language preference or settings.

  • Purpose: Improve user experience and functionality
  • Data Collected: User preferences and settings
  • Retention: 12 months

How to Manage Your Cookie Preferences

You can control your cookie preferences in the following ways:

  • Cookie Banner: Use the cookie consent popup that appears when you first visit our site
  • Manage Cookies Link: Click "Manage Cookies" in our website footer to change your preferences anytime
  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies. These include:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics (if analytics cookies are enabled)
  • EmailJS: For contact form functionality
  • Bootstrap CDN: For website styling and functionality

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

Your Rights

Under various privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and PIPEDA, you have rights regarding your personal data collected through cookies:

  • Access: Request information about what personal data we collect
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out: Withdraw consent or opt-out of certain data processing
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
